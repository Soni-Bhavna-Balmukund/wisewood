1. What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free return policy within 30 days of delivery. Please ensure the item is unused and in its original packaging for a full refund or exchange.

2. How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on your location and the product ordered. Typically, orders are processed within 1-3 business days, and shipping takes an additional 3-7 business days.

3. Do you offer assembly services?

Yes, we provide assembly services for select items. You can choose this option during checkout, and our team will assemble the furniture for you upon delivery for an additional fee.

4. Can I customize my furniture order?

Currently, we do not offer customization options for our furniture. However, we continually update our product catalog, so keep an eye out for new options.

5. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. Additionally, we offer secure payment through PayPal and other online payment platforms.

6. Do you offer warranty on your products?

Yes, we provide a limited warranty on all our products against manufacturing defects. Please refer to the warranty information provided with your purchase for details.

7. Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order has been processed and shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status of your delivery.
